UMass Lowell Rises to Top Research Classification

UMass Lowell is now listed as a Carnegie Research 1 (R1) university, a designation used to identify the nation's top research institutions based on their research activity. This is something only 7% of four-year universities in the United States can say about their commitment to research.

Meet the New Dean of the Francis College of Engineering

We're excited to welcome Susan Roberts to UMass Lowell as our new dean of the Francis College of Engineering! With UMass Lowell's recent Carnegie Research 1 (R1) designation and the launch of the Lowell Innovation Network Corridor (LINC), she sees many opportunities to elevate the college's reputation, strengthen it's community and expand opportunities for students, faculty and alumni.

Merit-Based Scholarships

Your students may be eligible for merit-based scholarships. Awards range from $5,000 to $20,000 and are renewable for four years (eight semesters). Your students are automatically considered for the scholarships they are eligible for—no special application is necessary.

International School Counselors and Recruiting Partners Visits

The University of Massachusetts Lowell is a welcoming and engaging community, and we are always excited to offer a tour of campus to our partners and school counselors based in other countries. It's the perfect way to see why our university is such a great place to refer international students.
