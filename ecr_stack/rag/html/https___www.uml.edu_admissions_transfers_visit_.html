Transfer Open House

Transfer Open House is the best way to figure out if UMass Lowell is the place for you. You'll get application help from admissions counselors, chat with faculty, discover the offices that will support you, tour campus, and more. This event is held each spring. Get notified about our next Transfer Open House, or other campus news and events.

Events for Transfer Students

Register for a transfer event to connect with us on campus or virtually:

  • Transfer Information Sessions – A great starting point to learn more about UMass Lowell (UML).
  • Application Help Sessions – Transfer counselors will be on hand to assist with application questions.
  • One-on-One Appointments – A transfer counselor will answer any transfer admissions questions.
  • On-the-Spot Decision Appointments – Transfer students with completed applications can register for one of these on-campus sessions.
  • Transfer Open House - Meet professional advisors, faculty and staff, tour campus, review the application and financial aid process. This event is held each spring. Get notified about our next Transfer Open House, or other campus news and events.

You can also request an alternate date to speak with a transfer counselor in person, by phone or on Zoom.
