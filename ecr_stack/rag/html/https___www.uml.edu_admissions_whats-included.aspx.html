As a top-tier public research university in Massachusetts, UMass Lowell (UML) is known for our focus on student success and outcomes. In fact, 96% of our graduates are employed or continuing their education. They’re also out there earning some of the highest starting salaries in New England.

That’s all extremely important for your future. But what about while you’re a student? Obviously, tuition covers the courses you take, but it also gets you tons of perks and discounts on top of that. Some “freebies” and discounts require a valid UML student ID.

For Your Entertainment

  • Free TV and movie streaming with Kanopy
  • Free tickets to all UML at-home sporting events
  • Free access to large scale board games, tabletop games and console games at the Gaming Zone
  • Student clubs and organizations and associated activities (some require dues)
  • Discounts at local restaurants, theaters, shops and activities
  • Discounts at museums in Boston
  • Discounts on ski and snowboard lessons, rentals and lift tickets at Pats Peak ski resort

For You to Experience

  • Kayak rentals at the UMass Lowell Bellegarde Boathouse
  • Lin Makerspace is a place where students in all majors go to create, invent and learn to use state-of-the-art technology, such as 3D printers, laser-cutting machines and more
  • Free fall welcome activities like Homecoming, the Engagement Fair and Convocation
  • The Association for Campus Events hosts activities like Southfest, Spring Carnival, Comedy Nights and more! The events are mostly free, but some events have associated costs.
  • Free public observing nights at the Schueller Observatory

  • Free laundry for campus residents
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Free library resources: Access to Libguides (database of academic journals), free ebooks via Libby
  • Free software and technology: Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office Suite, Zoom Premium, LinkedIn Learning
  • More resources available to on-campus residents

  • Free access to strength training, cardio equipment, group fitness classes, basketball courts and more at the Campus Recreation Center
  • Free physical therapy services
  • Stress relief fairs - free access to wellness activities before finals (baby animal petting zoo, therapy dog visits, chair massages, arts and crafts and more)
  • Access to counseling services ranging from individual counseling to group counseling and more 
  • On-campus Health Services
  • Free access to activities designed to help students unwind in the Serenity Center

  • The Advising Center provides free support from peer, professional and faculty advisors
  • DifferenceMaker Program is a campus-wide program that celebrates (and funds!) idea development and implementation for students who have creative and entrepreneurial mindsets
  • Free tutoring services for in-person or remote support
  • Free support on writing assignments from the Writing Center
  • Access to free, dedicated support, resources and opportunities for career-connected work with our Career and Co-op Center
  • Free networking events, like Career Fairs, that are split into specific fields to provide more targeted options for students and employers
  • Free access to thousands of internships and co-ops
  • Free reservations to meeting and study spaces throughout campus and the Club Hub
  • Free programs that help students transition to UMass Lowell (First-Year Orientation, Transfer Registration and more)

  • Free transportation with campus shuttles and free web app to track live bus locations, parking availability and more
  • Free daily bike rentals with the FreeWheelers Bike Share
  • Free access with student ID to the Lowell Regional Transit Authority (LRTA) for transportation needs outside of shuttle hours and to off-campus locations
  • Discounts for Zipcar membership
  • Discounts on Amtrak tickets
