Articulation agreements (also known as transfer agreements) have been developed with the intent to facilitate the transfer process for community college students to UMass Lowell. Please check the following list for agreements between UMass Lowell and community colleges outside of Massachusetts. Additionally, we have agreements with colleges and universities outside the U.S.

The following are active transfer agreements with UMass Lowell:

Maine

  • York County Community College
    • Criminal Justice (A.S.) (pdf)

New Hampshire

  • Great Bay Community College (transfer information)
    • Business (A.S) (pdf)
    • Criminal Justice (A.S) (doc)
    • Engineering (A.S.) (pdf)
  • Nashua Community College
    • Peace and Conflict (A.A.) (pdf)

More Information

For more information, contact a transfer counselor or advisor at your community college, or email us at: transfer@uml.edu.
