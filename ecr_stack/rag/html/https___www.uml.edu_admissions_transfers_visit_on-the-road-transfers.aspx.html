Transfer Counselors at Your College

UMass Lowell representatives will be visiting community colleges and attending transfer fairs both virtually and in person. Take a look at our scheduled programs. Events and details are added on an ongoing basis.

In addition to these transfer events, UMass Lowell also offers one-on-one appointments for transfer students.

Questions

If these dates and times don't work for you, or your school isn't listed, you can request an appointment. If you have any specific questions, please contact us or phone us at 978-934-3931.

Note: If you are a high school student, parent or counselor, please visit our Admissions On The Road webpage.
